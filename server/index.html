Overview

Capti Voice is a text-to-speech literacy tool designed to convert text to audio for a more accessible reading experience for its users. Currently Capti is being used in both K-12 and higher education settings. With its ability to read text-to-speech online as well as offline (through our standalone desktop app), Capti is the only TTS tool to work seamlessly across platforms and devices. By providing a free text-to-speech tool in the basic, individual account, it makes PDF’s, DAISY, EPUB, and a variety of other text formats more accessible to any user.

Capti’s focus on education makes it a wonderful text-to-speech program for both students and teachers. Its specialized educational features include OCR and Assignments, which allows a teacher or administrator to create a test right in Capti and attach it to a playlist for easy access to references. It’s easy to get in touch with an Outreach Manager to see a free demo and to discuss options for incorporating Capti Voice into your school!
